Our Story

Founded by Bldr. Bankayode Sunday Ibiyemi, Kayon Integrated Consults Ltd began with a simple vision: to deliver world-class construction, estate development, and facilities management services with integrity and excellence.

From small residential projects in Lokoja to large-scale commercial and estate developments across Nigeria, we have grown into a trusted name known for precision, innovation, and timely delivery.
